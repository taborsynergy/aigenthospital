🎥 How to Create the Video in HeyGen

Step 1: Create HeyGen Account
• Go to heygen.com
• Click "Start Free" (free trial + $25 credits)
• Sign up with email or Google
• Verify email
Step 2: Create New Video
• Dashboard → "Create" → "Avatar Video"
• Choose "Generate with AI" option
• Click "Continue"
Step 3: Select Avatar & Voice
Avatar Selection (Recommended):
• Choose avatar: "Josh" (professional, confident) or "Sarah" (warm, approachable)

Voice Settings:
• Language: English
• Voice: "US English - Male" (Josh) or "US English - Female" (Sarah)
• Speaking pace: 1.0x (normal)
Step 4: Paste Script
• Copy the script above (all text)
• Paste into HeyGen text box
• Review for any errors
• Click "Preview" to see timing
Step 5: Configure Settings
Video Format: 16:9 (landscape)
Background: "Professional Office" or "Blur"
Quality: 1080p (HD)
Duration: Should auto-calculate ~90 seconds
Step 6: Generate Video
• Click "Generate" button
• Wait 3-5 minutes (HeyGen processes)
• Video will appear in your dashboard
• Click preview to watch
Step 7: Download & Share
• Click "..." menu next to video
• Select "Download"
• Choose "1080p MP4" format
• Save as "Tabor_Synergy_Demo_90sec.mp4"

🔧 Troubleshooting

❓ "Video generation failed"

→ Check text doesn't have special characters (©, ™, etc.)
→ Verify internet connection
→ Try shorter text first (test run)

❓ "Avatar looks stiff/unnatural"

→ This is normal for AI avatars
→ Try different avatar (Josh vs Sarah)
→ Reduce speaking pace to 0.9x

❓ "Video is longer/shorter than 90 seconds"

→ HeyGen auto-calculates based on avatar speed
→ Adjust speaking pace: slower = longer
→ Or trim text: remove filler words

❓ "Low audio quality"

→ Upgrade to HeyGen paid plan for better voices
→ Or use Eleven Labs voice API (better quality)
