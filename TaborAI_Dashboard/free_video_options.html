Descript can generate a talking head video from text. Free tier has limited features but works.

Steps:

Combine Google's free text-to-speech with Canva's animated slides. Professional result, no avatars needed.

Step-by-Step (Takes 15 Minutes):

Step 1: Generate Audio (2 min)
• Go to cloud.google.com/text-to-speech
• Paste your script
• Select voice: "Standard English US Female" (natural sounding)
• Click "Play" → "Download MP3"

Save as: tabor_demo_voiceover.mp3
Step 2: Create Slides (10 min)
• Go to canva.com → Sign up (free)
• Create → "Video" (1280x720)
• Add slides with text:
   Slide 1: "Tabor Synergy — AI Medical Front Desk"
   Slide 2: "The Problem" (40% missed calls)
   Slide 3: "The Solution" (Aria features)
   Slide 4: "Live Demo" (placeholder)
   Slide 5: "CTA" (aifrontdesk.taborsynergy.com)
• Use brand colors: #1E40AF (blue), #059669 (green)
• Add simple transitions between slides
Step 3: Export & Combine (3 min)
• Canva: Export as MP4
• Download to computer

Option A (Easy - Use CapCut):
• Open CapCut (free app)
• Import: Canva video + Google TTS audio
• Sync audio to slides
• Export as MP4

Option B (No editing needed):
• Just use Canva slides with background music
• Add captions manually
• Still professional looking

Record yourself presenting on screen. Takes longer but often has highest engagement.

Steps:
1. Go to loom.com → Sign up free
2. Click "Start Recording"
3. Choose "Screen + Webcam"
4. Open your script on screen
5. Press record → read script + show demo on screen
6. Stop recording
7. Loom auto-saves, provides share link

✓ Benefit: Your authentic voice → higher trust + responses
