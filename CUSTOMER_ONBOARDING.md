## Section 9b — Your Embed Code

Once we create your clinic, we'll send you a snippet like this to paste into your website's `<body>` tag.
It works on WordPress, Wix, Squarespace, or any custom HTML page — no developer needed.

```html
<script
  src="https://aifrontdesk.taborsynergy.com/widget.js"
  data-clinic="your-clinic-slug"
  defer>
</script>
```

Your dedicated chat page will also be live at:
```
https://aifrontdesk.taborsynergy.com/c/your-clinic-slug
```
