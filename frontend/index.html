One config block — infinite specialties
Change your .env to deploy for any practice in minutes.
# Dermatology example
CLINIC_NAME=Sunshine Dermatology
SPECIALTY=Dermatology
AGENT_NAME=Nova
SERVICES_OFFERED=Skin checks, acne treatment, Botox, mole removal
PMS_SYSTEM=Modernizing Medicine (EMA)
PROVIDERS=Dr. Priya Mehta (MD), Dr. James Osei (PA)
INSURANCE_ACCEPTED=Aetna, BCBS, United, Cigna, Medicare

# Pediatrics example
CLINIC_NAME=Little Stars Pediatrics
SPECIALTY=Pediatrics
AGENT_NAME=Max
SERVICES_OFFERED=Well-child visits, sick visits, vaccinations, developmental screenings
PMS_SYSTEM=eClinicalWorks
