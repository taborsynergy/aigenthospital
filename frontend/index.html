🎨 White Label Licensing FAQ

Everything you need to know before purchasing the source code license.

What do I get with the $2,999 White Label license?

Full source code (FastAPI backend + frontend), Docker deployment setup, all EHR integration modules, 6 months of support, and the right to deploy as many instances as you need under your own brand. No per-seat royalties, ever.

Can I sell it to my own clients?

Yes. The White Label license includes reseller rights. You can deploy Aria for your own healthcare clients, charge them whatever you like, and keep 100% of the revenue.

Is the $2,999 a one-time fee?

Yes, one-time. You own the code. There are no recurring royalties or revenue shares. You will need to pay for your own hosting (Render, AWS, etc.) and your own OpenRouter/Anthropic API key.

Do I get future updates?

Yes — the license includes all updates for 12 months from purchase date. After 12 months you can renew at a discounted rate or keep the version you have.

What's the BAA situation for White Label?

We provide a BAA template. Since you self-host, you are the covered entity/business associate for your own deployments. We can provide the BAA template and HIPAA documentation package on request.

How do I get started?

Book a 30-minute technical discovery call. We'll confirm your use case, answer any remaining questions, and send a commercial agreement to sign before payment.

2

Fill in your practice name, specialty, address, phone, office hours, providers, and accepted insurance. This is how Aria learns about your clinic.

3

Chat with Aria using your clinic's information. Ask it your most common patient questions. Tweak the setup until the answers feel right.
