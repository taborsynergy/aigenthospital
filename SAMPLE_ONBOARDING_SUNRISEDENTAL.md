## SECTION 5 — PATIENT REGISTRATION

**Required fields:**
Full Name, Date of Birth, Gender, Contact Number, Email, Home Address,
Emergency Contact Name + Phone, Insurance Provider + Member ID + Group Number,
Dental history (last visit date, known allergies, current medications)

**New patient process:**
Aria collects: full name, DOB, contact number, email, insurance details, and reason for visit.
Aria books the first available new-patient slot (60 min) and sends confirmation.
Patient receives new patient forms link via email before the visit.

**Returning patient process:**
Aria verifies: full name + DOB. Pulls up existing record. Books next available slot.
If it has been 6+ months, books as a recall appointment (cleaning + exam).

**HIPAA verification:**
Full name + Date of birth + Last 4 digits of SSN

## SECTION 8 — ESCALATION & EMERGENCY

**Emergency symptoms → immediate 911 redirect:**
- Severe jaw swelling spreading to throat or neck (airway risk)
- Uncontrolled bleeding after extraction (30+ min)
- Signs of allergic reaction to anesthetic (hives, swelling, difficulty breathing)
- High fever with facial swelling (signs of spreading infection/abscess)
- Facial trauma from accident with possible jaw fracture

## SECTION 9 — TOP FAQs

**Q1: Do you accept Delta Dental insurance?**
A1: Yes, we are in-network with Delta Dental. We bill your insurance directly and you only pay your co-pay at the time of visit.

**Q2: How much does a teeth cleaning cost without insurance?**
A2: A routine cleaning is $120. For new patients, the exam + X-rays + cleaning package is $199.

**Q3: Do you see children?**
A3: Yes, we see children from age 3 and up. We recommend a first visit by age 1 per ADA guidelines.

**Q4: Can I get an emergency appointment today?**
A4: We reserve same-day slots for dental emergencies (severe pain, broken tooth, lost crown). Call (512) 555-0199 first thing in the morning — slots fill quickly.

**Q5: How long does Invisalign take?**
A5: Average treatment is 12–18 months depending on your case. Dr. Chen offers a free Invisalign consultation. Book one today!

**Q6: Is teeth whitening covered by insurance?**
A6: Cosmetic procedures like whitening are not covered by dental insurance. Our in-office whitening is $450 and take-home kits are $250.

**Q7: What happens if I miss my appointment?**
A7: A $75 no-show fee applies. We understand things come up — please call at least 24 hours ahead to reschedule at no charge.

**Q8: Do you offer payment plans?**
A8: Yes, we accept CareCredit with 0% financing for 12 months for procedures over $500.

**Q9: How do I get my X-rays sent to another dentist?**
A9: We can email your X-rays and records to any provider. Just ask our front desk or tell Aria — we'll send within 24 hours.

**Q10: Is there parking?**
A10: Yes, free parking is available in the building garage on Sunrise Blvd. Validated for 2 hours.
